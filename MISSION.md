# Mission

Help founders publish clearer, more credible LinkedIn posts by giving agents reusable review workflows that identify where founder readers may stop before the post goes live.

## Principle

AI can draft a post. A good review workflow should test whether the intended reader would keep reading.

## Core Wedge

Founder-reader judgment:

- Where does the reader stop?
- What feels generic?
- What feels credible?
- What context is missing?
- What should be cut, moved, or clarified?

## What We Avoid

- Engagement guarantees.
- Virality promises.
- Algorithm optimization claims.
- AI detection claims.
- Ghostwriting by default.
- Generic social media growth advice.
