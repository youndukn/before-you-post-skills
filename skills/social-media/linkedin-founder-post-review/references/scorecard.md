# Scorecard

Use 1-5 scores. Be strict but practical.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Blocks reading or trust |
| 2 | Noticeably weak |
| 3 | Usable but not sharp |
| 4 | Strong with minor edits |
| 5 | Ready for the intended reader |

## Dimensions

- Hook
- Audience fit
- Skimmability
- Specificity
- Credibility
- Human voice

## Overall Verdict

Use one:

- Ready to post
- Needs light edits
- Needs structural edits
- Needs a clearer point

Do not include engagement predictions.
